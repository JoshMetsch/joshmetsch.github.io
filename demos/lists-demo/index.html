Date Carmel

Ingredients

  • 20 pitted Medjol dates
  • 2/3 to 3/4 cup hot oat milk
  • 1 teaspoon vinilla extract
  • pinch of sea salt (optional)

Instructions

  1. Soak the dates place pittd dates in a bowl and cover them with hot water. let them soak for about 15-20 minutes. This will soften them, making it easier to blends them into a smooth carmel.

  2. Drain the dates after soaking, drain the dates of the water.
